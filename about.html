About

Born and raised in Aspen, Colorado, I graduated from Middlebury College with a Bachelor of Arts in Neuroscience and a minor in Computer Science.

My love for art started at a young age and my work is often inspired by the places I go and the people I meet along the way.

I created a portfolio of acrylic paintings during my senior year of high school. Each piece was given as a gift of appreciation to someone who greatly influenced my life.

While at Middlebury, I explored other mediums including photography, multiple types printmaking, and computer generated artwork.
